Tabbit

Overview

Tabbit is an all-in-one management app designed for small business owners. It streamlines day-to-day tasks that often feel overwhelming and mundane, reducing time spent on administration. Tabbit allows users to reclaim their creativity and focus, resulting in greater business efficiency and less stress.

Current Problem

Not enough creativity and too much logistics.

Being surrounded by creatives looking to sell their work  (including myself). By starting at markets and occasionally moving their business to a website, which is not as common. Due to how complicated it is to run an online business and a business in general. Compared to vending, where you have to mass-produce once every few months, and you sell it all at once.


The commitment to running a business can be daunting, on top of the numerous factors to consider:


  • whether the business is profitable

  • inventory management

  • business growth

  • lack of benefits (if gone full time)

  • book keeping

  • taxes

  • customer information

  • marketing and social media

  • shipping logistics

  • time management

  • price management

  • product creation (the extensive effort that goes into creative products)


That’s only to name a few factors.
