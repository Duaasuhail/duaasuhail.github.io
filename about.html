About

I use design, empathy, and community-powered thinking to craft products that actually make sense (and spark joy). Years of gaming taught me that every problem has a clever solution you just need the right strategy. Learning and growing, learning code,and learning new skills.

Hey, I’m Duaa.

I’m a third-year student at York University’s Design program. Growing up I have always been a creative I’ve been illustrating and drawing for over two decades. With a passion for problem solving I realized product design was for me very quickly with a side of coding of course ;).

My goal? Communicate effectively and solve problems through creativity, innovation, and continuous learning especially in design and coding.



Design Values

Learning is growing.

As a young designer I embrace the mistakes I make as apart of my process. Each slipup is an opportunity to reflect adapt and move closer to my goals.

Efficiency is key.

While working through different design projects, I’ve learned how important it is to streamline and simplify the process. How it not only creates clarity for the designer but also the user.

Community is the foundation of everything.

Not only is collaboration and feedback something I treasure. They’re practices I push for in design, sharing ideas helps designers grow. As the saying goes it takes a village!
