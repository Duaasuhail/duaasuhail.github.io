Current Problem

Loneliness and the lost art of hobbies.

Hobbies are being reframed as another form of productivity or monetization rather than pure enjoyment. How there is a guilt around spending time doing something for their enjoyment rather than trying to make money constantly. When hobbies are crucial to maintaining healthy mental health. Which helps create space for creativity, self expression and relaxation. Combatting loneliness by providing conversation topics and social connection between humans.

Problem Statement

How might we design a platform for millennials to build new hobbies while fostering a community and connections.

Goals

1.

Help millennials find a sense of community.

2.

Push for millennials to indulge in hobbies.

Research Phase

Background

I interviewed 4 millennial gardeners ranging from beginner to extremely experienced.

Asking the right questions:

  • • Do you think you have a green thumb?

  • •What are the major issues you face when gardening?

  • • How attached are you to your plants?

  • • Where do you find information to care for your plants?

What did I find?

  • • 50% encountered their plants dying often (no green thumb still a newbie)

  • • 75% also have friends who garden.

  • • 100% would love to show off their plants progress.

  • • 75% of interviewees were interested in gamification and a trophy system.

User Research

Gardening is someone's interest! Who though?

Using the information from the interviews and the studies done online regarding millennials I created two user personas. Focusing on those in the workplace who don’t have that same opportunity post-secondary students do to connect with others.

Design System

Therapeutic, relaxing and welcoming.

The main goal with this branding was to create a calming environment where users could come after work and not feel stressed because of the big bright colours other apps use. Instead I wanted to lean into the calming tones that plants hold.

Conclusion

What I Learned

The importance of an IA.

Before using an IA I had no idea how to keep track of my design. Creating an IA with features and pages creates a clear cut understanding of what I needed and what the user needed. As well as what I was designing.

Assumptions are the downfall of designs.

I am not a millennial so being able to speak with millennials and get their thoughts and understand their way of thinking helped so much and cleared up any assumptions I had made previously. Being able to empathize with the user helped created t he perfect app design for them.
